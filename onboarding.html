Pricing Model
Step 5 of 10 — Rate structure from the Spark Membership–PayArc contract
No action needed on this step. Pricing is applied automatically by the Spark backend using a pre-configured PayArc pricing template (apply_pricing_template_id, from the PayArcApplyPricingTemplateId setting). School admins never see or set rate values — so there is no pricing selection here.
The real add-lead payload attaches the configured pricing template; no pricing fields are collected from this form.
